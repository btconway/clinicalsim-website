## The Challenge

Breaking bad news is a core clinical skill, yet most clinicians have never practiced it before doing it for real.

## Key Insights

- Modern training solutions need to reach all learners across a program, not a handful per rotation cycle.
- Every trainee deserves the same quality of practice opportunity.
- Validated feedback mechanisms are what drive real skill development.
- Training should be available when and where learners need it.

## The Research

Recent research in medical communication training reinforces the value of structured, repeatable practice. Communication failures drive 40% of malpractice claims, and HCAHPS scores put millions in reimbursement at risk. Most programs still lack the infrastructure to train at the scale these numbers demand.
